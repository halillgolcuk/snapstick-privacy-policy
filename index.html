SnapStick Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

This policy describes how the SnapStick Chrome extension (“the extension”) handles information when you use it. SnapStick is built so that sticker creation and your library stay on your device. There is no SnapStick account and no SnapStick backend that receives your tab captures or stickers.

What the extension does

SnapStick lets you capture the visible area of the active browser tab (when you start the feature), select a region or subject, and produce transparent PNG stickers. You can save stickers in the extension, copy them to the clipboard, or download them.

Data stored on your device

The extension may store locally (via Chrome’s extension storage):

  • Sticker images you create and keep in your library
  • Metadata needed to show the library (for example previews and identifiers)
  • A recent capture used to resume editing when you open the flow
  • Minimal UI state (for example an optional badge count)

This data remains on your computer inside your browser profile unless you remove it (for example by deleting stickers in the extension or uninstalling the extension).

What is not collected by SnapStick

SnapStick does not:

  • Send your tab captures, stickers, or library contents to SnapStick servers (there are none for this purpose)
  • Sell personal data or use it for advertising
  • Read your browsing history as a list of visited URLs
  • Track you across websites for analytics
  • Ask for your name, email, or login for the extension to work

Permissions (why they exist)

  • activeTab — Only when you start sticker creation: capture the visible tab you are using and run the in-page editor on that tab.
  • scripting — Inject the packaged in-page editor on the active tab for that flow.
  • offscreen — Run image processing in an extension offscreen context so work can finish reliably.
  • storage — Save stickers and small extension state locally.
  • clipboardWrite — Only when you choose Copy, write the sticker image to the clipboard.

Network / third-party services

On first use, the segmentation model file used for assisted cut-out may be downloaded from Google’s model hosting used by MediaPipe (for example storage.googleapis.com). That download is a model asset, not your page content. Your captures and stickers are not uploaded to SnapStick. Processing runs in the extension’s contexts on your device.

Chrome itself and your operating system may handle updates and security as usual; that is outside this extension’s control.

Clipboard

If you use Copy, the extension writes the sticker PNG to the system clipboard so you can paste it into other apps. The extension does not read your clipboard contents.

Children’s privacy

The extension is not directed at children under 13, and it is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy when the extension or legal requirements change. The “Effective date” at the top will be updated when we do.
